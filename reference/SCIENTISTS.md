# SCIENTISTS.md

> Influential Computer Scientists Reference for the Master Computer Science Handbook (MCSH)

---

# Purpose

This document provides a curated reference of influential scientists whose work has fundamentally shaped Computer Science.

Rather than serving as a collection of biographies, this document identifies the historical contributions, research impact, and relationships between scientists and the topics covered throughout the handbook.

Every referenced scientist should have a lasting influence on the discipline rather than temporary industrial popularity.

---

# Design Principles

Each scientist entry should emphasize:

- Historical context
- Primary contribution
- Lasting impact
- Related research fields
- Landmark publications
- Related handbook volumes

Avoid personal details that are not academically relevant.

---

# Standard Entry Format

Every scientist should follow the structure below.

---

## Full Name

## Related Concepts

---

## Related Volumes

---

## Historical Importance

Explain why this scientist permanently changed Computer Science.

# Citation Guidelines

When introducing a major concept:

- Mention the scientist naturally.
- Explain the historical motivation.
- Reference the landmark contribution.
- Avoid lengthy biographies.

Historical context should support understanding rather than interrupt technical explanations.

# Final Goal

This document provides a shared reference for the influential individuals whose ideas shaped modern Computer Science.

By consistently connecting concepts to their originators, the handbook helps readers understand not only the technical content, but also the intellectual history and evolution of the field.
