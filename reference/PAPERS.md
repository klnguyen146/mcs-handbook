---

# Purpose

This document curates the most influential research papers in Computer Science that are referenced throughout the Master Computer Science Handbook.

The objective is **not** to provide an exhaustive bibliography.

Instead, it identifies the foundational publications that established major theories, algorithms, architectures, and paradigms across Computer Science.

These papers represent milestones that every graduate-level Computer Science student should recognize.

---

# Design Principles

Paper selection follows five criteria:

- Historical significance
- Long-term academic impact
- High citation influence
- Foundational contribution
- Relevance to graduate education

Recent papers should only be included if they have clearly influenced the direction of the field.

# Recommended Reading Strategy

Readers are **not** expected to read every paper in full.

Suggested progression:

1. Read the chapter.
2. Understand the intuition.
3. Review the engineering implementation.
4. Read the paper abstract.
5. Study the methodology.
6. Return to the chapter with deeper understanding.

# Maintenance Policy

New papers should only be added if they satisfy at least one of the following:

- Introduced a new research paradigm.
- Established a widely adopted method.
- Received lasting academic recognition.
- Became foundational in graduate education.

Temporary trends should not be included.

# Final Goal

The purpose of this document is to provide readers with a curated roadmap of the most influential research papers in Computer Science.

Rather than overwhelming readers with exhaustive bibliographies, this reference highlights the publications that fundamentally changed the discipline and continue to shape modern research and engineering practice.
