# VENUES.md

> Academic Publication Venues Reference for the Master Computer Science Handbook (MCSH)

---

# Purpose

This document defines the major academic conferences and journals referenced throughout the Master Computer Science Handbook.

Its objective is to help readers understand where influential Computer Science research is published, how different research communities are organized, and which venues are considered authoritative within each subfield.

This document is intended as an academic orientation rather than a ranking.

---

# Design Philosophy

Publication venues are selected based on:

- Academic reputation
- Long-term impact
- Community recognition
- Historical significance
- Relevance to graduate education

The handbook should reference venues that consistently publish influential research over many years.

# Usage Guidelines

When citing research:

- Mention the venue if it provides useful academic context.
- Avoid presenting venue prestige as the primary reason for importance.
- Focus on the scientific contribution of the work.

Venue names should support readers in navigating the research landscape.

# Maintenance Policy

New venues should only be added if they demonstrate sustained influence within the Computer Science research community.

Temporary workshops or short-lived conferences should generally not be included.

---

# Final Goal

This document provides readers with a structured overview of the major publication venues in Computer Science.

It enables graduate students to understand where important research is communicated, how research communities are organized, and how different areas of Computer Science evolve through their leading conferences and journals.
