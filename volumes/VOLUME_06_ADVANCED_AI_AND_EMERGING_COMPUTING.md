# Purpose

This volume explores the frontier of modern Artificial Intelligence and emerging computing paradigms.

Rather than teaching fundamental AI algorithms, this volume focuses on state-of-the-art models, large-scale intelligent systems, trustworthy AI, distributed AI, graph learning, multimodal intelligence, scientific AI, and future research directions.

Readers should develop the ability to understand recent AI papers, evaluate new techniques, reproduce research results, and identify promising research opportunities.

---

# Target Audience

This volume is intended for readers who:

- Have completed Volumes 01–05.
- Understand Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and NLP.
- Want to conduct AI research.
- Plan to pursue a Master's thesis or PhD.
- Build production-scale AI systems.
